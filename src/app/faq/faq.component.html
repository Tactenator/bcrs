Frequently Asked Questions

User Configuration - Delete User

Step 1 : Sign In As Administrator [See "Account Registration" for Details]

Step 2 : Click "User Management" Tab

Step 3 : Click "Trash" Icon for User you wish to Delete

Step 4 : Confirm Delete [CANNOT BE UNDONE]

 User Configuration - Edit User

Step 1 : Sign In As Administrator [See "Account Registration" for Details]

Step 2 : Click "User Management" Tab

Step 3 : Click "Pencil" Icon for User you wish to Edit

Step 4 : Edit User Information as desired and click "Update User"

 User Configuration - Create New User

Step 1 : Sign In As Administrator [See "Account Registration" for Details]

Step 2 : Click "User Management" Tab

Step 3 : Click "Create User" Button

Step 4 : Fill in all Information

Step 5 : Click Submit and See Your User Added to the List

 Account Registration

Step 1 : Click Sign In Button

Step 2 : Click Register Button

Step 3 : Fill in Required Information

Step 4 : Create Security Questions

Step 5 : Click Submit and then "Sign In" as normal

 Reset Password

Step 1 : Click Sign In Button

Step 2 : Click "Forgot Password"

Step 3 : Enter your email and hit "Submit"

Step 4 : Answer your Security Questions and hit "Submit"

Step 5 : Enter New password and hit "Submit"
